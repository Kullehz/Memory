MemoryRoots: Stories of the Past for the Future

The project's purpose is to help alleviate existing societal problems in the Philippines, particularly concerning the deceased. Currently, there is a significant lack of accessible information regarding many individuals who have passed away. This issue creates difficulties not only for families seeking to trace their lineage but also for institutions that require accurate records. To address this gap, our project proposes the development of a comprehensive and organized database dedicated to preserving and providing information about the deceased. Through this program, we aim to ensure that the memories and histories of those who came before us are properly recorded, easily accessible, and preserved for future generations. Ultimately, our goal is to honor the departed while fostering a deeper connection between past, present, and future communities. Database that helps people identify information about the deceased.
